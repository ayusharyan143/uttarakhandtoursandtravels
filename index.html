Nainital

  • Nainital is a beautiful hill station surrounding a pear-shaped lake.

  • Key attractions include Naini Lake, Naina Devi Temple, Snow View Point, and The Mall Road.



    • Mussoorie

  • Known as the "Queen of Hills," Mussoorie is a charming hill station with stunning landscapes.

  • Popular attractions include Gun Hill, Kempty Falls, Camel's Back Road, and the Mall Road.


    • Rishikesh

  • Famous for its spiritual significance and adventure activities, Rishikesh is situated on the banks of the Ganges River.

  • Visitors come for activities like white-water rafting, yoga, and to visit the famous Triveni Ghat and the Beatles Ashram.
